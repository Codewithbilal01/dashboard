About Us

HVAC Specialist Duct Solutions is a premier duct cleaning service provider serving residential and commercial clients across Canada. With over 15 years of industry experience, we are committed to improving indoor air quality and creating healthier living environments.

Our certified technicians use state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly cleaning solutions to remove dust, allergens, and contaminants from your HVAC systems. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service with attention to detail and customer satisfaction.
